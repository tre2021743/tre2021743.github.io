The Story

DoubleU

Born from the neon-soaked transit of the 405, DoubleU creates the soundtrack for the drive home. His sound is a collision of atmospheric synths and raw, percussive energy.

From the foundational tones of 2021 to the 2026 LP Faded 405, DoubleU marks a definitive shift in electronic storytelling. built for those who find clarity in the blur of the fast lane.
